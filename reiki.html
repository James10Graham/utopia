What happens before and during a session?

You will contact me and let me know what you would like help with and if you have had previous experience of reiki. An appointment will be arranged to come to your home. The area and energy in the room is cleared. Depending on length of session you will either lay on the portable bed provided or sit on a chair. Reiki Music will be offered to enable the atmosphere. It is best you are free from distractions in your home during this time. Reiki involves the transfer of universal energy from practitioner to patient/client using hands lightly on or over specific area of the head torso and limbs for between 2-3 minutes per are sometimes longer depending on the length of session and area of concern. In the simplest form it works with the energy fields around the body.
