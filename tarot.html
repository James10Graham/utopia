Tarot

Tarot cards is a practice that allows the user (me)to gain insight into the past present and future of the person requesting the reading. This can help in many ways; you may require guidance or help on the following:

  • love / emotional issues or questions
  • Financial concerns
  • Career

or you can get a general reading

I offer a few different types of readings depending on your location. For a reading please use the request a reading contact card with the type of reading you require, and I will get back to you.

In-Person

If you reside in the bb10, bb11, bb12 area I can come to you. This can be done as a single booking.

For postcodes within a ten mile radius, I offer group readings – you can get a group together min of five people max of eight people ( I require a separate room for this as some readings are sensitive) I clear the space and you each will take turns in having a reading.

Virtual

Email

Email reading full spread – once I receive request, I will ask for your location name and age, this is purely so that I am connecting to the right persons energy when doing the reading. The type of reading you require will be discussed then and I will let you know how to pay. Once payment is made, I book you in at the earliest possible time and let you know when you will receive your reading.

Telephone

Telephone reading- Once I receive a request for reading I will let you know the timescale and how to pay once this has been received I will book you in at the earliest possible time and call you at your appointment time, the only thing I ask is that you make yourself fully available and free from distractions. I will call you at your appointment time, there is no need to do anything else.

Our paths do change, That is what gives us the excitement and challenges we face and you are in full control of how you utilize the advice of the cards, We can change our path and destiny with every decision we make.
